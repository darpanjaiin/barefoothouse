üöó How to Reach

Barefoot House is located away from all the hustle-bustle of the popular hill-stations in a small village called Purkul.

  • Nearest Railway Station (Dehradun): 14 kms / 30 mins

    Regular trains available from Delhi, Lucknow, and other major cities. Pre-book your taxi from the station for convenience.

  • Nearest Airport (Jolly Grant): 40 kms / 01 hr

    Daily flights from Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore. Airport taxis and pre-paid services available at the terminal.

  • Distance from Mussoorie: 20 kms / 45 mins

    Take the scenic route via Mussoorie Road. Perfect for a day trip to the Queen of Hills.

  • Distance from Rishikesh: 60 kms / 1.5 hrs

    Easily accessible for those planning to combine their stay with spiritual and adventure activities in Rishikesh.

A private car parking is available within the premises.
