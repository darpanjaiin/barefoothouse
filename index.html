Things To Do

Nearby Destinations

Visit to Dhanolti

Nestled in the serene hills of Uttarakhand, Dhanolti is a tranquil escape from the bustling cities. Wander through the lush deodar forests, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and soak in breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Explore quaint local markets and indulge in regional cuisine for an authentic experience.

Visit to Mussoorie

Just a short drive away, Mussoorie offers a charming mix of colonial architecture and modern attractions. Stroll along Mall Road, visit the iconic Kempty Falls, and enjoy panoramic views from Gun Hill. Spend a day exploring this vibrant hill station and return to the peaceful ambiance of Barefoot House.

Visit to Landor

A hidden gem, Landor is perfect for those seeking solitude. Experience the beauty of this quaint village, known for its picturesque landscapes and historical significance. Enjoy leisurely walks through the lush greenery and discover the local culture.

Visit to Surkanda Devi Temple

Embark on a spiritual journey to the Surkanda Devi Temple, perched at an altitude of 2,900 meters. The trek offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. For an added thrill, take the cable car ride to the temple, gliding above the picturesque landscape.

Nature & Adventure

Treks to Jhabarkhet Nature Reserve

Explore the untouched beauty of Jhabarkhet Nature Reserve, ideal for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. The treks offer a chance to connect with nature, observe diverse flora and fauna, and enjoy the serene environment.

Jungle Treks

Experience the thrill of jungle treks, guided by local experts. Venture through hidden trails and discover the rich biodiversity, while learning about the ecosystem and its inhabitants.

Exclusive Experiences

Private Singer Gigs and Bonfire Session

Unwind under the starlit sky with a private singer gig. Enjoy soulful melodies while gathering around a cozy bonfire, sharing stories and creating lasting memories.

Barbecue Session

Indulge in a delightful barbecue session, grilling fresh local produce and meats. Enjoy this interactive dining experience surrounded by nature.

Picnic Breakfast

Start your day with a delightful picnic breakfast in scenic surroundings. Enjoy a carefully curated meal amidst nature, with the sounds of birds chirping and gentle rustle of leaves.

Yoga Session

Rejuvenate your mind and body with a calming yoga session amidst peaceful surroundings, led by experienced instructors while enjoying stunning mountain vistas.

Special Arrangements

Make your special occasions unforgettable with tailor-made arrangements. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or any celebration, we offer personalized services for cherished memories.

Barefoot House is not just a place to stay; it's an experience to cherish. With a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural exploration, your time here will leave you rejuvenated and inspired.
